Environmental Impacts of Food Production

Although food is essential for humans, its production is a massive environmental concern. In recent years, there has been a lot of attention to meat production, which directly contributes to sustainability issues such as carbon dioxide emissions. However, there are also concerns within the crop production industry, which poses a threat to biodiversity as well as contribute largely to the issues posed by meat production, namely land and water.
